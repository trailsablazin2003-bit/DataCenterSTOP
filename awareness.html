Display a Yard Sign
Displaying a yard sign is one of the most visible and effective ways to show that a community is united. When many homes display the same message, it sends a clear signal that the issue matters to the entire town, not just a few individuals.
  • Show visible support in your neighborhood
  • Help demonstrate community-wide agreement
  • Encourage others to learn more and get involved
Sign the Petition
Petitions help show decision-makers that the community is paying attention and wants to be heard.
  • Display our collective support
  • Encourage friends and family to add their names
Share on Social Media
Posting about the issue helps spread awareness quickly. When sharing, include our website so others can learn more.
  • Post on Facebook about the proposed data center
  • Use hashtags like #SaveMarshfield and #DataCenterSTOP
  • Encourage others to share and get involved
Talk to Your Community
Conversations in schools, churches, and local events help keep the issue visible and encourage more people to get involved.
  • Talk with neighbors about the issue
  • Bring it up at community gatherings
  • Stay informed about local meetings
